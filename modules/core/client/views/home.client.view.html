What is LendAHand?

LendAHand is an application that creates a personal network of freinds and mutual friends where you can request and provide services ranging from asking somone to deliver you food, to finding someone who can fix your car.

Standard Benefits of LendAHand

  • Avoid being scammed or overcharged by only dealing with known peers.
  • Open negotiation between parties resulting in lower prices and incorperating bartering aspects such as other forms of payment.
  • Discover skills of your friends that you may have never known
  • Always be able to have a personal reference
